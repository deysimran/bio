Simi's Page


Books? naah, not me. I’ve never been that study all day kind of girl. I’m the one who’d rather be out there, running, playing, laughing, feeling the wind hit my face while I’m chasing a ball instead of chasing grades. Cricket, Football, Badminton, Basketball. I don’t even choose, I just play. That’s where I feel most like me. I get bored sitting still. I need energy, movement, something real. Books make me sleepy, but games make me alive.

And don’t even ask about my phone because yeaa, I scroll way too much. Insta is literally my comfort zone. Reels, Memes, Random Edits that somehow match my mood at 3am. I love taking pictures, capturing random little moments that feel like memories in motion. I click a lot but never really post much. Some things just look better when they stay private.

Music is my world. I could listen to it all day, every day. It’s my escape, my therapy, my safe place. I love singing too, sometimes quietly, sometimes loud when no one’s around. And dancing just feels like freedom to me. I’ll dance when I’m happy, I’ll dance when I’m lost. It’s how I let go, how I feel alive again.

But there’s also that side of me nobody really sees. The one that writes poems. Little thoughts, Random feelings, Midnight notes that turn into verses. I write when I can’t say things out loud. Sometimes it’s messy, sometimes it’s deep, but it’s always real.

I’m not a studious girl, I’m a vibe. A sporty soul with a poetic heart, a lazy brain with a camera roll full of unposted memories. I live for moments, not marks. I scroll too much, laugh too loud, sing off key, dance like no one’s watching, and play every game like it’s the last one.

So no, I’m not a perfect student. I’m a chaos with a playlist, a dreamer with a notes app full of poems, a voice that sings when no one’s listening, and a heart that’s always a little louder than it should be.
